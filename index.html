Ice Cream

Whether the weather is over 100 degrees fahrenheit or below freezing point (32 degrees fahrenheit), everyone likes ice cream. According to https://www.icecream.com/icecreaminfo, 87% of America has ice cream in their freezer at any time. Ice cream is a delightful frozen treat that everyone likes. In the olden days, ice cream was sold in a truck that comes down a street playing a song to catch your attention. Nowadays, you usually get it in a store or at an ice cream shop.

Why It Is Called Ice Cream

A long time ago, people used ice to cool down sweet cream or custard in order to make ice cream. It was first named "iced cream". It then changed into "ice cream" in 1744.

The History

The earliest form of ice cream was very different than the one we see today. The Greeks might have been the ones to first have an form of ice cream which is also known as an icy drink flavored with honey. Types Of Ice Cream

There are many different kinds of ice cream. A few most known ones are mochi, gelato, soft serve, and sherbert.
